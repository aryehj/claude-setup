## Label rubric

| label | definition |
|-------|-----------|
| `science` | Peer-reviewed paper, preprint, clinical guideline, systematic review, or content from a university/govt research page |
| `editorial-considered` | Long-form, bylined editorial from a named journalist or expert; Substack post with analysis; policy brief; trade journal |
| `seo-fluff` | Generic informational page with no original analysis; exists primarily to rank for search traffic |
| `listicle` | "Top 10...", "Best X...", numbered list without substantive individual entries |
| `marketing` | Product/service promotion, affiliate content, brand blog with a sales agenda |
| `other` | Wikipedia, forum post, social media, video, directory, or anything that doesn't fit the above |

## Acceptance criteria

For at least one query, a (prompt × categories × prior) combination must raise
`science + editorial-considered` share by **≥30 percentage points** and lower
`seo-fluff + listicle + marketing` share by **≥30 pp** vs the baseline variant.
